Profile

Software Engineering

Seven years of development experience, five of them in Python. My secondary languages are Scala, Java and JavaScript.

Through test-driven developement I strive to build clean and maintainable code. Build automation tools and version control are standard parts of my toolbox.

I have experience in building apps and REST APISs with Django and Flask and I don't hesitate to dive into HTML5 and CSS. Am happy to discuss different database types or the latest deployment and provisioning tools.

Architecture is key

Data Analysis, Mining & Visualization

As an expert in the PyData stack, I'm able to perform complex ETL operations, quickly explore new datasets and rapidly prototype models. Scraping and data cleaning are my guilty pleasures.

Machine learning and Bayseian statistics are areas where I have a secure foundation and would like to further cultivate, model validation is not an afterthought for me.

Visualizing as a means of communication, but also for insight discovery is central to my workflow.

Visualizing enables understanding

Entrepreneurial and Design Thinking

I believe that constant testing and validated learning are the key to success. Formulating hypothesis requires empathy, intellectual humility and a broad and open mind. This and putting value for the user first are my fundamental principles.

With hands-on marketing experience and education in business areas such as strategy, finance and accounting, I am ready to join in on any conversation and emphasize in B2B contexts.

About Me

I hold a BSc. Wirtschaftsingenieurwesen which covers areas from electrical engineering and computer science to economics and business administration. During a one year scholarship at the University of California, Berkeley, I discovered my passion for Data Science and proceeded to write my graduation thesis at the artificial intelligence department of the Technical University Berlin.

I've recently beta launched dasgeht, a cultural events aggregator with a focus on user experience.
Go check it out!
When I'm not programming I like to go bouldering, cook, read (mostly non-fiction) and throw funny moves on the dance floor. Born and raised in Freiburg, Germany and have since lived in Berlin, Oakland, New York and traveled to over 20 countries.
